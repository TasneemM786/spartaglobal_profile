Education

University of East London, BA Fine Arts 2015-2017

Modules include: Studies in Cinematic, Painting, Printmaking, Sculpture, Printmaking, Photography, Multimedia, Textiles, Ceramics, Art History and also a Research Thesis based on Art Psychotherapy of the Syrian children refugees suffering from post traumatic disorders and other mental health disorders.

A Fine Art graduate who has completed three years with a year abroad in California State University of Monterey Bay with an overall mark of an Upper Second Class Degree. This included adapting in the working world of solo exhibition events at a professional level; gaining and evolving in management and coordination skills, keeping organised and planning events, formally. Also working with different mediums and materials, exploring different aspects in Arts and learning about different cultures and people. Being able to grow and have an open mind, thinking outside the box to help bring out more artistic ideas and creativity. Exploring different aspects in life and writing a thesis or dissertation on the Syrian Civil War. Working with primary research on Psychotherapy for the traumatised children of Syria, dealing with post-traumatic stress disorders; receiving a first class grade.
